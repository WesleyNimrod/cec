DBR (Debt Burden Ratio)

  • 50% for Salaried customer
  • 30% for Pensioners
  • DBR can go up to 60% for salaried customers with any UAE National Housing Programs. DBR excluding the EMI for Housing program should not exceed 50% in such cases. Documentary proof is required to confirm that customer is a beneficiary of UAE National Housing Program. Above is not applicable to Pensioner customers.
  • Additionally, DBR can go up to 50% specifically for Pensioner customers with Sheikh Zayed Housing Program. DBR excluding the EMI for Sheikh Zayed Housing Program should not exceed 30% in such cases. This is only applicable to Pensioners who have availed Sheikh Zayed Housing Program and documentary proof is required to confirm that customer is a beneficiary of Sheikh Zayed Housing Program.
